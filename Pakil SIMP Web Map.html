Pakil Social Impact Assessment (SIA) Interactive Map

The Social Impact Assessment (SIA) was undertaken to establish a comprehensive baseline of Pakil's current socioeconomic conditions prior to the implementation of the Pakil Pumped Storage Hydroelectric Power Project (PPSHPP). Central to this assessment was the household socioeconomic survey, which gathered detailed data on demographics, income levels, employment, education, and access to basic services, among others, across all barangays.
